Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a listing cost?

Basic listings are free during our launch period. Premium listings with enhanced features start at $15/month. Elite packages with homepage placement and analytics are $99/month.

How long does review take?

We typically review and publish new listings within 48 hours. Premium listings get priority review within 24 hours.

Can I edit my listing after it's published?

Yes! All listing owners can request updates to their information, photos, and hours at any time. Premium members get a self-service dashboard.

What information do I need to provide?

At minimum, we need your cafe name, address, category, and a high-quality photo. We also recommend providing phone number, opening hours, social media links, and a description of what makes your cafe special.

How do you verify listings?

We verify listings by cross-referencing Google Maps data, calling the business, and in some cases visiting in person. Premium verified listings display a gold badge on their page.

Can I remove a listing?

Yes. If you are the business owner and want your listing removed, please contact us via this form or WhatsApp and we'll process it within 24 hours.
